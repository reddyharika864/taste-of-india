Hyderabadi Biryani

Description: Hyderabadi Biryani is a world-famous dish made with aromatic basmati rice, tender meat, and rich spices. It is a symbol of Hyderabad's culinary heritage.
Ingredients:
  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or Mutton
  • Yogurt
  • Saffron
  • Onions
  • Spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, etc.)
Making Process:

Marinate the meat with yogurt and spices. Partially cook the rice and layer it with the meat. Cook on dum (slow heat) for perfect blending of flavors. Serve hot with raita or salan.
