Tamil Nadu Famous Foods

Idli

Description: A soft, fluffy steamed rice cake that is a staple breakfast in Tamil Nadu.
Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Urad Dal
  • Fenugreek Seeds
  • Salt
Making Process:

Soak rice and urad dal with fenugreek seeds. Grind to a smooth batter and ferment overnight. Steam in idli molds and serve hot.
