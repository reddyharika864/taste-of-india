Bisi Bele Bath

Description: A spicy and flavorful rice dish made with lentils, rice, and a unique blend of spices, typically served hot.
Ingredients:
  • Rice
  • Toor Dal (Pigeon Pea Lentils)
  • Vegetables (Carrots, Beans, Peas)
  • Bisi Bele Bath Masala
  • Tamarind
  • Ghee
  • Cashews
Making Process:

Cook rice and toor dal separately. Prepare a mixture of tamarind, masala, and vegetables. Combine everything and simmer, garnished with ghee and cashews. Serve hot.
