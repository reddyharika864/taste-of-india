Kerala Famous Foods

Puttu

Description: A traditional steamed rice cake layered with coconut, often served with banana or curry.
Ingredients:
  • Rice Flour
  • Grated Coconut
  • Water
  • Salt
Making Process:

Mix rice flour with water and salt to make a crumbly mixture. Layer it with grated coconut in a puttu maker and steam until cooked.
