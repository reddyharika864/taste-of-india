Chhattisgarh Famous Foods

Faraa

Description: Faraa, a traditional rice dumpling dish, is a staple breakfast in Chhattisgarh. It is steamed and lightly seasoned, making it healthy and delicious.
Ingredients:
  • Rice flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Green chilies
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
Making Process:

Prepare a soft dough with rice flour and water. Shape into small dumplings and steam. Temper with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies before serving.
