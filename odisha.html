Odisha Famous Foods

Pakhala Bhata

Description: A traditional fermented rice dish served with fried or mashed vegetables and fish. It is a summer staple in Odisha.
Ingredients:
  • Cooked rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Green chilies
  • Curd (optional)
Making Process:

Soak cooked rice in water overnight to ferment. Serve it cold with salt, green chilies, and optional curd. Pair it with fried vegetables or fish curry.
