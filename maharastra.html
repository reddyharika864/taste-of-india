Maharashtra Famous Foods

Vada Pav

Description: Known as the "Indian Burger," Vada Pav is a popular street food in Maharashtra made with a spicy potato fritter placed inside a pav (bread roll).
Ingredients:
  • Potatoes
  • Gram Flour (Besan)
  • Garlic
  • Green Chilies
  • Pav (Bread Rolls)
  • Spices and Chutneys
Making Process:

Boil and mash potatoes, mix with spices, and shape into balls. Dip in gram flour batter and deep fry. Serve hot inside pav with chutneys.
