Poha Jalebi

Description: Poha Jalebi is a beloved breakfast dish in Madhya Pradesh, featuring flattened rice (poha) tempered with spices and served alongside sweet, crispy jalebi.
Ingredients:
  • Flattened rice (Poha)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Crispy jalebis
Making Process:

Rinse poha and drain. Temper mustard seeds, green chilies, and onions in oil. Add turmeric and mix in the poha. Serve hot with freshly prepared jalebis for a perfect sweet and savory combination.
