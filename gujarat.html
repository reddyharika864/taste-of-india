Gujarat Famous Foods

Khaman Dhokla

Description: Khaman Dhokla is a soft and fluffy steamed snack made from gram flour. It is a quintessential Gujarati delicacy, often served as a breakfast or tea-time snack.
Ingredients:
  • Gram flour (Besan)
  • Yogurt
  • Green chilies
  • Mustard seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Eno fruit salt
Making Process:

Mix gram flour with yogurt, water, and spices to make a batter. Add Eno fruit salt to aerate the batter. Steam the batter in a greased tray and temper with mustard seeds and green chilies. Serve with green chutney.
