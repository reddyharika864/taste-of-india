Andhra Pradesh Famous Foods

Pulihora

Description: A tangy and spicy tamarind rice dish that is a popular offering during festivals and celebrations in Andhra Pradesh.
Ingredients:
  • Cooked Rice
  • Tamarind Pulp
  • Green Chilies
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Curry Leaves
  • Groundnuts
Making Process:

Cook rice and let it cool. Prepare a tempering with mustard seeds, green chilies, curry leaves, and groundnuts. Add tamarind pulp, mix with rice, and serve.
